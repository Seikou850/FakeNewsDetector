About Me

I am Seikou a student at City of Westminster College and I make this website to DETECT FAKE NEWS that circulate in social media and internet. This website is made to combat the information that circulates around us. WHAT ARE FAKE NEWS? Fake news deliberately spreads false information. They try to attract attention with supposedly (authentic) content to shock and manipulate public opinion. This type of news is created by both individuals and groups, acting in their own interest or on behalf of others. The spread of disinformation has primarily personal, political or economic objectives.
